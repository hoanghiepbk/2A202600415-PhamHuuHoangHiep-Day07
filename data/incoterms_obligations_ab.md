# Incoterms 2020 - A/B Obligations Quick Notes

Source: ICC Incoterms 2020 (compiled from `incorterm.md`)
Language: English
Domain: Incoterms
Section: obligations

Incoterms present obligations in mirrored article pairs:

- A1/B1 General obligations
- A2/B2 Delivery and taking delivery
- A3/B3 Transfer of risk
- A4/B4 Carriage
- A5/B5 Insurance
- A6/B6 Delivery/transport document
- A7/B7 Export/import clearance
- A8/B8 Checking, packaging, marking
- A9/B9 Allocation of costs
- A10/B10 Notices

## Why this matters for retrieval

This structure allows precise lookup:

- questions about "who does what" map to A/B obligations
- questions about "who bears risk" map to A3/B3
- questions about costs map to A9/B9

## Typical query examples

- Under rule X, who performs import clearance?
- Under rule X, when does risk transfer?
- Under rule X, who pays unloading costs?
