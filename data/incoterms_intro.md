# Incoterms 2020 - Introduction Notes

Source: ICC Incoterms 2020 (compiled from `incorterm.md`)
Language: English
Domain: Incoterms
Section: introduction

## What Incoterms 2020 do

Incoterms rules define a common interpretation of trade terms used in sale contracts.
They mainly clarify:

- obligations of seller and buyer
- where and when risk transfers
- how costs are allocated between parties

## What Incoterms 2020 do NOT do

Incoterms are not a contract by themselves and do not replace a sale contract.
They do not regulate:

- whether a valid sale contract exists
- transfer of ownership/title of goods
- payment terms and currency
- remedies for breach
- dispute resolution law/method/venue

## How to incorporate correctly

Best practice format:

`[chosen rule] [named place/port] Incoterms 2020`

Examples:

- `CIF Shanghai Incoterms 2020`
- `DAP [named destination] Incoterms 2020`

Including the year is important to avoid ambiguity between versions.
The named place/port is critical because it affects delivery point, risk transfer, and cost allocation.
